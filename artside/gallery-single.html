AFROCENTRIK

This series is much more than a simple visual representation, it is a vibrant celebration of African cultural richness, its diverse identities and its deep-rooted heritage. Through bold palettes and patterns inspired by ancestral traditions, Afrocentrik explores the themes of pride, spirituality and belonging (ujamaa). Powerful and intimate, each piece of Afrocentrik reflects a personal and universal quest, recalling the importance of our roots and heritage. This collection is part of an artistic approach that seeks to raise awareness, inspire and enhance the beauty and identity of Africa in a modern multicultural context where we feel like we are lost in Western cultural influence.
